My Student UX/UI Portfolio

I'm a current User Experience (UX)/User Interface (UI) student learning under the University of Minnesota in partnership with edX. I am really good at centering user empathy, completing primary user research, and conducting nuanced qualitative research. My focus is on organizing designs for a satisfying experience. I design with the fundamental understanding of what it means to be user-centered and human-centered. Something about me, I have come to appreciate the failures I experience. Those are the times when I get a chance to enjoy exploring the possibilities and apply problem solving.
